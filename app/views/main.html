What We Do

The Law Firm of Elliot J. Wiesner, P.C. provides personal and quality services to its clients. We keep our clients fully informed of the status of their case and strive to return all phone calls within 24 hours.

The Firm represents clients from individuals to businesses with sales of millions of dollars a year. Matters are handled on a flat fee, hourly, or contingent basis. All credit and debit cards are accepted.

  • Business Law, Commercial Litigation and Collections
  • Family Law
